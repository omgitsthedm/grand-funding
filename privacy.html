Privacy Policy

Plain-language privacy for borrowers, brokers, and website visitors.

Last updated March 10, 2026. This page explains what Grand Funding LLC collects through this website and how that information is used.

Website inquiries Pre-approval forms Arizona + California lending

What we collect

When you call, email, or submit a form, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, property details, loan scenario information, and any other details you choose to provide.

How we use it

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, evaluate potential lending opportunities, communicate next steps, and improve the experience of this website. We do not collect more than we need to review a request and follow up.

Form submissions and communications

Website form submissions are processed through Netlify Forms and routed to Grand Funding LLC for follow-up. If you contact us by email or phone, your message may be retained to continue the conversation and document your request.

Analytics and site operations

This website may use analytics or basic server logs to understand site usage, page performance, and lead activity. Those tools help Grand Funding LLC understand which pages are useful and whether form and call-to-action flows are working properly.

Sharing and retention

Grand Funding LLC does not sell personal information submitted through this website. Information may be shared with service providers involved in hosting, analytics, or communications only as needed to operate the site or process your request. Information may be retained for business, legal, compliance, or record-keeping purposes.

Your choices

  • You can call instead of using a form at (602) 935-0371.
  • You can request updates or corrections by emailing info@grandfundingllc.com.
  • If you do not want to provide optional details, leave those fields blank and share only what is necessary for follow-up.

Contact

Questions about this policy or about information you submitted through the website can be sent to info@grandfundingllc.com or directed by phone to (602) 935-0371.
