Arizona Construction Loan FAQs

Common questions from Arizona builders and developers

What is an Arizona construction loan?

+

An Arizona construction loan is short-term financing for ground-up new builds or major renovations. Funds are released in milestone-based draws as construction progresses and inspections confirm completion. Grand Funding offers Arizona construction loans up to $5M with 24-hour approvals and closings in 5-10 business days.

How do construction draws work in Arizona?

+

Construction draws are released in stages as milestones are completed. You submit a draw request, an inspection confirms the work, and funds are released for the next phase. You only pay interest on the funds already drawn — not the full loan amount.

Do I need an experienced GC for an Arizona construction loan?

+

A licensed general contractor is required. The GC's experience, license, and track record are important factors in our underwriting. We look at the project plans, the contractor's portfolio, and the exit strategy when evaluating Arizona construction loan applications.

What is the maximum Arizona construction loan?

+

Arizona construction loans are available up to $5,000,000. This covers single-family new builds, multi-family construction, luxury renovations, ADUs, and larger commercial projects across Arizona.

How long does it take to close an Arizona construction loan?

+

Most Arizona construction loans close in 5-10 business days from loan approval. The timeline depends on title clearance, plans review, and appraisal. Grand Funding moves as fast as your documentation allows.
