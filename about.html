Our Story

Grand Funding was built for borrowers who do not have time for slow committees, vague answers, or lenders who do not understand the asset. We work with investors, builders, and business owners who need a lender that can assess the property, the plan, and the exit in the real world.

Our background in lending, construction, and development changes how we underwrite. We understand budgets, draw timing, permitting friction, value-add strategy, and what can derail a project. That lets us move quickly without pretending every deal fits a conventional box.

We help Arizona and California borrowers with:

  • Bridge, fix-and-flip, construction, land, rental, and cash-out loans
  • Second-position and business-purpose structures
  • Construction draws shaped by real project experience
  • Direct communication from first call through closing

We focus on equity, leverage, and exit strategy instead of treating the credit score as the whole story. When a deal fits, we move. When it doesn’t, we tell you clearly and early.

That approach is why experienced borrowers come back to us. They know they will get a real conversation, a practical structure, and a team that understands how deals actually get done.
