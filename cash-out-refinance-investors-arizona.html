Arizona Investor Cash-Out Refi FAQs

Straight answers for working investors

What is a cash-out refinance for investors in Arizona?

+

An investor cash-out refinance in Arizona replaces your existing mortgage with a new, larger loan — you receive the difference in cash. Grand Funding's program is asset-based, lends up to 75% LTV, requires no income docs, and closes in 7-12 days. The new loan is secured by the investment property.

What LTV can I get on an Arizona investor cash-out refinance?

+

Up to 75% LTV based on the current appraised or market value of the investment property. For example: a $600K property qualifies for up to a $450K cash-out refi loan. No income verification — approval is based on property equity and exit strategy.

How fast does an Arizona investor cash-out refinance close?

+

7-12 business days is typical. The additional time (vs. a bridge loan or purchase) accounts for title work and payoff coordination with the existing lender. We drive the process hard — the timeline is determined by title speed, not ours.

Do I need income docs for an Arizona investor cash-out refinance?

+

No. Grand Funding's cash-out refi is asset-based — we underwrite on property value and equity, not W-2s or tax returns. Self-employed investors, LLC borrowers, and those with complex income qualify exactly the same way as any other borrower.

What Arizona property types qualify for investor cash-out refinance?

+

Single-family rentals, 2-4 unit multifamily, commercial investment properties, and equity-rich fix-and-flip projects. Investment and non-owner-occupied properties only — primary residences are excluded from this program.

What can I use the cash-out funds for?

+

Anything deal-related: down payments on new acquisitions, rehab budgets, paying off high-rate debt, working capital, or bridging to the next opportunity. Grand Funding doesn't restrict use of funds — we just need a clear exit strategy for the refinanced property.
