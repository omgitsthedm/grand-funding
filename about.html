Our Story

At Grand Funding, we don't just underwrite loans—we understand what's behind them. Homes under construction. Properties being flipped. Developers solving complex timelines. With decades of experience in lending, building, and deal structuring, we bring a hands-on, real-world perspective most lenders can't.

We've been in the field. We've managed projects, walked job sites, dealt with permitting delays, and lined up subs. That background gives us an edge. We know how to spot risk, structure a clean deal, and move fast without cutting corners.

We Serve Arizona and California with:

  • Hard money loans for flips, builds, and land
  • Construction draws with real support
  • Second-position and cross-collateral loans
  • Bridge financing and business capital

We underwrite based on equity and exit—not just credit scores. We pick up the phone. We move fast. And we give straight answers, even when the answer is"no."

Thousands of borrowers have trusted us to fund their deals—and not just because we're fast. It's because we know what it takes to get from plans to payoff.
